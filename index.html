Welcome to Pokemon Hunter

Game Instructions:

  • You start the game with 5 pokeballs.
  • You have 60 seconds to catch them all.
  • After some quantity of moves or after passing some amount of time, you will lose pokeballs. So try to use less moves and hurry up!!!
  • You need at least 3 pokeballs to win the level.
  • Press the Start button to begin.
  • Gotta catch 'em all!!! Good Luck!!!
